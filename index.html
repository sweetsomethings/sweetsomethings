A little about us

Hi, I'm Ashley!

A sweet treat lover transforming my kitchen into an Arizona cottage bakery. Sweet Somethings started with batches for friends and family, and it's growing one dessert at a time.

I believe the little somethings create the sweetest moments. Everything is baked from scratch with real, simple ingredients.

Baking is my happy place — and I'm so glad you're here ♡

Tips from our kitchen

  • Use room temperature ingredients
  • Measure flour the right way (spoon & level!)
  • Don't overmix the dough
  • Chill dough when you can — flavor loves patience
  • Bake with love. Always.

Perfect pairings

  • Warm cookie+ cold milk
  • Lemon crinkle cookie+ hot tea
  • Warm cinnamon roll+ hot coffee
  • Any bake at all+ someone to share it with ♡
